Attribution · How we verify

How Push Attribution Works

Three independent signals per conversion. QR scan + receipt timestamp + merchant confirmation. Stripe Connect underneath. Push never holds money.

Six-step mechanism

From merchant signup to creator payout.

01

Merchant signup

Merchant signs up on Push. Choose Attribution Lite (free) / Essentials ($99/month) / Pro (Year 1 outcome-based).

02

Creator posts

Creator posts about merchant on Instagram / TikTok / Xiaohongshu with Push QR code.

03

Campaign binding

Each post carries a Push QR code bound to creator × merchant × campaign. Unique identifier per content piece.

04

QR scan at checkout

Customer scans QR at checkout (or staff scans receipt). Timestamp recorded instantly.

05

Triple verification

Push records the conversion: QR scan + receipt timestamp + merchant confirmation. All three signals cross-checked.

06

Creator payout

Creator is paid per verified conversion via Stripe Connect. Push takes rev share (15–20%). No MSB/FinCEN exposure.

Three independent signals

How we verify each conversion.

01

QR scan

Customer physically scans at point of sale. Requires physical presence. Cannot be gamed remotely. Device fingerprint + IP + timestamp locked.

02

Receipt timestamp

Merchant system logs the transaction timestamp. Cross-references with QR scan time within acceptable window. Prevents retroactive claims.

03

Merchant confirmation

Merchant confirms the visit. Third check against both QR and receipt record. Detects collusion and false claims. Logged to dispute record.

Creator earnings

How creators get paid.

Creator earns $8–25 per verified conversion depending on tier. Push takes 15–20% rev share on payout. All settled via Stripe Connect. Push is never an MSB/FinCEN entity.

FTC compliance

Attribution that respects the law.

Every attribution event is verified, disclosed, and decaying. FTC 16 CFR Part 255 requires that paid creator relationships be disclosed. Push generates compliant records by design — with attribution decay built in (100%/50%/30%/10%/0% per recency window). Legacy ad platforms do not provide this. We do.

What we're not claiming

100% attribution doesn't exist. 95% decision confidence does.

Every ad platform fakes the precision of their attribution. We don't. When a customer walks into a café, they may have seen 12 posts, 3 ads, and a friend's text. We claim only the one signal we can prove — and we tell merchants the confidence level so they make informed decisions. This honesty is the product.
