DECISION RULES

Four Rules for Reading the Data

When signals conflict, these rules clarify where the real problem lives. Don't treat symptoms — treat root causes.

IF merchant repeat is low but campaign fill is high
→
THEN problem is likely campaign outcome quality, not supply quantity
IF creator signups are high but completion is low
→
THEN supply quality is weak, not top-of-funnel volume
IF standby usage is high but fulfillment quality drops
→
THEN urgency is working, but screening is weak
IF support load rises faster than completed campaigns
→
THEN workflow is not scaling cleanly

METRICS TRAPS

Four Traps That Corrupt Decisions

Good metrics discipline means knowing what NOT to celebrate as much as what to celebrate.

A
TRAP A
Celebrating Signups

Signups without completion or repeat are weak. A surge in creator or merchant registrations that doesn't convert to completed campaigns and repeat usage is a vanity metric — it reveals nothing about platform health.

B
TRAP B
Overvaluing Views

Views are not the core business outcome. Content reach and impressions are secondary effects. The core business outcome is a verified redemption that a merchant found valuable enough to repeat. Views without attribution to transactions are decorative.

C
TRAP C
Ignoring Support Cost

A campaign that works only with heavy manual intervention is not healthy. If support cost per completed campaign is rising faster than volume, the workflow is not scaling — it's being manually propped up. High GMV with high support load is not healthy growth.

D
TRAP D
Mistaking Repeat for Quality

Repeat matters only if economics remain healthy and trust remains high. A merchant who repeats because they have no better option — not because the platform delivered real value — is not a retention win. Monitor repeat rate alongside contribution margin and dispute rate together.

THE FINAL STANDARD

What Good Dashboards Do

The purpose of measurement is not to describe the company — it is to discipline it.

Clarify where the system is getting stronger. Expose false progress quickly. Distinguish volume from quality. Help the team make sharper, not noisier, decisions. Good dashboards do not just describe the company — they discipline it.

看出真进展和假繁荣的区别。分清是单量涨了还是质量好了。哪个数字在涨、哪个在跌，打开就知道。
