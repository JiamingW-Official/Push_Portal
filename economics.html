Push Economics: Attribution Rail Unit Economics

Merchants pay per verified store visit. Creators earn per conversion. Push takes a rev share. All on Stripe Connect.

(Per-conversion economics)

How the numbers work.

Creator earnings
$8–25 per verified conversion. Scales by creator tier (Tier 1 $8, Tier 5+ $25).
Push rev share
15–20% of payout. Stripe Connect handles all settlement; Push never holds money.
Verification + ops
Multimodal AI (QR + receipt + geo). On-demand arbitration reserves. Included in rev share.
Merchant ROI
Real-time verified attribution. Weekly ROI card. Zero transaction risk (Stripe handles settlement).

(Operating principles)

How we keep the system fair.

Stripe Connect
All payouts flow through Stripe. Push never holds money. Creators and merchants retain full control of their funds.
Attribution decay
100% / 50% / 30% / 10% / 0% over time. Repeat visits require new verification. FTC-compliant.
Creator tier progression
6 tiers from Bronze to Obsidian. Earnings scale with verified conversions, audience growth, and engagement quality.
Zero intermediary risk
Merchants fund campaigns directly to creators' Stripe accounts. Push is the infrastructure layer, not a payment hub.
