问题

三方困局，市场失灵

商家之痛

无法衡量达人ROI。投放预算盲目，无法追踪从内容到到店的转化路径。钱花了，不知道效果。

达人之痛

缺乏定价权。没有标准化的能力证明，报价全靠感觉，优质达人被劣质达人挤压。

市场之痛

供给充沛但无结构化渠道。大量达人和商家需求存在，但缺乏匹配、验证和履约的基础设施。

English

The Problem

A three-sided market failure

Merchant Pain

Cannot measure creator ROI. Ad spend is blind with no way to track conversion from content to foot traffic. Money spent, results unknown.

Creator Pain

Lack pricing power. No standardized proof of ability means pricing is guesswork. Quality creators get undercut by low-quality ones.

Market Pain

Abundant supply, no structured channel. Massive creator and merchant demand exists but lacks infrastructure for matching, verification, and fulfillment.
