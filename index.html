Local businesses burn money on creators.

You DM ten creators. Three ghost. Four ask for $200 and a free meal. Two post something that looks good. None of them bring verifiable customers to your door. You can't tell which worked. So next month you do it again, or you quit.

BEFORE PUSH

Sort creators by follower count. Guess who fits your brand. Hand out free food. Wait for posts. Screenshot some likes. Hope.

Monthly spend: $600–$1,500. Verifiable customers acquired: unknown. Repeat rate: you stop after 3 months.

WITH PUSH

You hire an outcome.

Tell us: "I want 50 new customers through the door this month." AI picks creators from our vetted network. They run the campaign. QR codes verify visits. You approve. You pay per verified customer.

Monthly spend: $2,000 for 50 customers. Verifiable customers: each one QR-verified. Repeat rate: 72% run a second month.
