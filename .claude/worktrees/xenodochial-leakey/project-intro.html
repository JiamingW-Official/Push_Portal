PUSH 是
  • 交易系统
  • 信任系统

PUSH 不是
  • 网红市场平台
  • 创作者发现应用
  • 忠诚度系统

English

PUSH is a local creator performance infrastructure layer. We help merchants acquire customers through measurable, creator-driven demand.

PUSH is a transaction system, trust system, creator credit system, and local demand orchestration system.

PUSH IS
  • Transaction system
  • Trust system
  • Creator credit system
  • Local demand orchestration
PUSH IS NOT
  • Influencer marketplace

⚠️
Core Problem
核心问题

中文

商家端痛点

  • 无法判断创作者质量
  • ROI 不清晰，效果难以衡量
  • 运营摩擦大，流程复杂

创作者端痛点

  • 缺乏定价权
  • 收入不稳定
  • 经常遇到压价和剥削性报价

市场问题

创作者供给未被结构化地应用于本地商业。

English

Merchant Pain Points

  • Cannot judge creator quality
  • Unclear ROI, hard to measure outcomes
  • High operational friction, complex workflows

Creator Pain Points

  • Lack pricing power
  • Income instability
  • Exploitative and lowball offers

Market Problem

Creator supply is not structured for local commerce.

💡
Core Insight
核心洞察

中文

创作者是非结构化的客户获取渠道。

机会在于将创作者驱动的需求进行：

  • 标准化 — 统一规范与流程
  • 验证 — 确认真实性与质量
  • 排名 — 按绩效分级
  • 路由 — 智能匹配供需

English

Creators are unstructured customer acquisition channels.

The opportunity is to take creator-driven demand and:

  • Standardize — unified processes and norms
  • Verify — confirm authenticity and quality
  • Rank — tier by performance
  • Route — intelligently match supply and demand

五大战略支柱
📜
Pillar 01
Offer OS

Performance-based reputation and scoring system that builds trust and unlocks better opportunities.

🌐
Pillar 04

🔀
Pillar 05
Liquidity Layer

中文

这五大支柱共同构成了 PUSH 的核心架构：

  • 报价OS — 结构化报价、定价和交易流管理
  • 创作者信用 — 基于绩效的声誉和评分系统
  • 归因分析 — 将创作者行为与商家成果透明关联
  • 需求界面 — 本地需求的呈现、组织和可执行界面
  • 流动性层 — 确保商家需求与创作者供给的持续匹配

English

These five pillars form the core architecture of PUSH:

  • Offer OS — structured offer creation, pricing, and deal flow
  • Creator Credit — performance-based reputation and scoring
  • Attribution — transparent tracking of creator-to-merchant outcomes
  • Demand Surface — where local demand is organized and made actionable
  • Liquidity Layer — consistent matching of supply and demand at scale
