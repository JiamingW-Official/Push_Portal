# Push Brand Voice & Messaging — Quick Reference

## 1. Brand Personality

Push speaks like a **sharp, confident operator** — not a corporate marketer, not a hype startup.

### Five Core Traits

| Trait | Definition | NOT |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| **Direct** | Say the thing. No filler, no hedging. | Not rude or dismissive |
| **Credible** | Lead with data, proof, outcomes. | Not boastful or exaggerating |
| **Empowering** | You can do this. We help you do it better. | Not condescending or hand-holding |
| **Ambitious** | We're building infrastructure, not a feature. | Not delusional or overpromising |
| **Human** | Real language, real problems, real people. | Not robotic or corporate-speak |

**The Push Test:** Read it aloud. Does it sound like a smart, direct friend helping you make money? If yes, it's Push. If it sounds like a SaaS landing page or LinkedIn motivational post, rewrite it.

## 2. Tone Spectrum

Push shifts tone by audience but keeps personality constant.

| Audience | Tone | Energy | Example |
|----------|------|--------|---------|
| **Merchants** | Practical, results-focused | Calm confidence | "You spent $200 last month on creators. Here's what you got." |
| **Creators** | Encouraging, growth-oriented | Energetic, grounded | "You completed 5 campaigns. Score 65. Proven tier unlocked." |
| **Investors** | Strategic, data-driven | Controlled intensity | "We don't sell software. We sell verified customers." |
| **Press/Public** | Bold, visionary | High conviction | "The creator economy has 200M people and zero infrastructure." |
| **Team** | Honest, no-BS | Real talk | "Repeat is 28%. Below target. Here's what we're changing." |

## 3. Messaging Architecture

### Level 1: One-Liner (5 sec)
> Push turns creators into measurable customer acquisition for local businesses.

### Level 2: Elevator (30 sec)
> Local businesses waste money on creators they can't measure. Push fixes that — we match merchants with verified local creators, track every customer via QR attribution, and build a performance score so the best creators rise to the top. Merchants pay for results, not guesses.

### Level 3: Full Narrative (2 min)
> There are 200 million creators and countless local businesses that need customers. But the connection is completely broken — all DMs, guesswork, zero measurement.
>
> Push is the infrastructure layer that makes this work. A merchant tells us their goal — say, 20 new customers this week. Our system matches them with local creators, tracks every customer through QR attribution, and builds a performance history so results improve over time.
>
> For creators, Push is the first platform where your commercial results actually matter. No followers required to start. Complete campaigns, build your score, unlock better pay — from free coffee at Seed to $150/campaign as Partner.
>
> We're starting in one NYC neighborhood — café, dessert, beauty. Prove the loop, then scale. Every campaign makes matching smarter. That data is our moat.

## 4. Writing Rules

### Always
- Lead with outcome, not feature
- Use specific numbers ("$19.99/month" not "affordable")
- Active voice ("Push tracks" not "Customers are tracked")
- Short sentences. Punch.
- "You" language for merchant/creator

### Never
- Don't start with "At Push, we..." (boring, self-centered)
- Don't use "revolutionize" / "disrupt" / "game-changing"
- Don't say "influencer" — say "creator"
- Don't say "platform" externally — say "engine" or "system"
- Don't use ! in merchant/investor copy (desperate)
- Don't hedge ("we believe") — state it ("Push is...")

### Formatting
- **Headlines:** Short, punchy, no period
- **Body:** 2-3 sentence paragraphs max
- **CTAs:** Action-first ("See your ROI" not "Click here")
- **Emails:** Get to point in line 1. No "I hope this finds you well."

## 5. Brand Vocabulary

### Words We Own

| Word | Context |
|------|---------|
| **Push Score** | Creator's commercial performance rating |
| **Seed / Explorer / Operator / Proven / Closer / Partner** | The 6 creator tiers |
| **Verified acquisition** | What we sell to merchants |
| **Performance infrastructure** | What Push IS |
| **Creator credit** | System that ranks creators by results |

### Competitive Language

| We Say | Not | Why |
|--------|-----|-----|
| "Acquisition engine" | "Marketplace" | Engine = intelligent. Marketplace = replaceable. |
| "Verified results" | "Brand awareness" | Results = measurable. Awareness = unmeasurable. |
| "Performance-scored" | "Vetted" / "Curated" | Scored = data-driven. Vetted = subjective. |
| "Local creators" | "Influencers" | Creator = operator. Influencer = vanity. |
| "Infrastructure" | "Platform" / "Tool" | Infrastructure = indispensable. Tool = switchable. |
