## For Merchants

**Core promise:** Stop guessing. Start measuring.

### Pain → Solution Framing

| Pain Point | Push Message |
|-----------|-------------|
| "I don't know if creators work" | "Every customer tracked. Every creator scored. See your exact ROI." |
| "It's too expensive" | "$19.99/month. That's less than one Instagram ad." |
| "I don't have time to manage this" | "You set the goal. We handle matching, execution, and verification." |
| "How do I know they'll show up?" | "Creators build a performance score. Unreliable ones get filtered out." |

### Key Phrases to USE
- "verified customer acquisition"
- "track every visit"
- "performance-scored creators"
- "repeat with one click"
- "your ROI dashboard"

### Phrases to AVOID
- "influencer marketing" (too generic, bad associations)
- "brand awareness" (unmeasurable)
- "collab" / "partnership" (sounds casual, not transactional)
- "exposure" (worthless framing)

## For Creators

**Core promise:** Your results are your currency. Build them here.

### Pain → Solution Framing

| Pain Point | Push Message |
|-----------|-------------|
| "I'm too small for brand deals" | "No follower count needed. Start as Seed, earn your way up." |
| "I never know what I'll earn" | "Clear pay per campaign. Plus commission on every sale you drive." |
| "Nobody tracks my real impact" | "Your Push Score proves your commercial value. It's your portfolio." |
| "How do I get better opportunities?" | "Higher score = higher tier = better pay + premium brands." |

### Key Phrases to USE
- "earn from day one"
- "your Push Score"
- "level up" / "unlock"
- "commission on every sale"
- "performance is your portfolio"

### Phrases to AVOID
- "side hustle" (diminishing)
- "free products" as the main pitch (Seed is the starting point, not the destination)
- "followers needed" / "minimum following" (Push's entire point is NO follower gate)
- "apply now!" (sounds like a job ad)

## For Investors

**Core promise:** Outcome-as-a-service for local commerce.

### Narrative Structure

1. **Problem:** 200M+ creators, zero infrastructure for local commerce. DMs don't scale. Agencies are expensive. Ads can't do attribution.
2. **Insight:** Creators are unstructured customer acquisition channels. Structure them = new market layer.
3. **Solution:** AI-native engine. QR attribution. Performance scoring. Autonomous matching.
4. **Moat:** Two-sided performance data that compounds. Competitors can use the same AI but not our data.
5. **Traction:** [Insert current numbers — repeat rate, completion rate, merchant WTP]
6. **Ask:** [Funding amount] to expand from 1 ZIP code to 5.

### Killer Lines for Investors

- "We don't sell software. We sell verified customers — delivered by AI-matched creators."
- "Every campaign makes our matching engine smarter. That's a data moat no one can copy without running the same campaigns."
- "In SaaS winter, we're not selling seats. We're selling outcomes."
- "Shopify built the rails for e-commerce. We're building the rails for local creator commerce."

### Numbers to Always Lead With

- Merchant repeat rate (THE PMF signal)
- Creator completion rate
- Unit economics (35% margin target)
- Cost comparison vs alternatives ($19.99 vs $500-2000 agency)

## For Social Media

### Platform-Specific Tone

| Platform | Style | DO | DON'T |
|----------|-------|-----|--------|
| **Instagram** | Visual, aspirational but grounded | Show real creator content, real merchants, real results | Generic motivational quotes, stock photos |
| **TikTok** | Fast, authentic, behind-the-scenes | Day-in-the-life of Push campaigns, creator success stories | Overproduced, corporate feel |
| **LinkedIn** | Strategic, thought-leadership | Industry insights, founder journey, market analysis | Humble-bragging, "thrilled to announce" |
| **Twitter/X** | Sharp, opinionated, concise | Hot takes on creator economy, data points, contrarian views | Thread bait, engagement farming |

### Content Pillars

1. **Proof** — Real campaign results, real numbers, real merchants
2. **Creator stories** — Progression journeys, tier unlocks, earning milestones
3. **Industry insight** — Creator economy problems, local commerce trends
4. **Behind the build** — Honest founder content, challenges, learnings
