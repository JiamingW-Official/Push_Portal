PUSH

The system that standardizes local creator-driven customer acquisition.

🌎
01 — INTRO
Vision

PUSH is a local creator performance infrastructure layer. We help merchants acquire customers through measurable, creator-driven demand.

PUSH is a transaction system, trust system, creator credit system, and local demand orchestration system.

PUSH IS
  • Transaction system
  • Trust system
  • Creator credit system
  • Local demand orchestration
PUSH IS NOT
  • Generic influencer marketplace
  • Local discovery app
  • Content community
  • Reward app
  • Lightweight loyalty system
⚠️
02 — PROBLEM
Core Problem

Merchant Pain Points

  • Cannot judge creator quality
  • Unclear ROI, hard to measure outcomes
  • High operational friction, complex workflows
  • Fear of being burned by no-shows or low quality

Creator Pain Points

  • Lack pricing power
  • Income instability
  • Exploitative and lowball offers
  • Trust asymmetry with merchants

Market Problem

Creator supply is not structured for local commerce.

💡
03 — INSIGHT
Core Insight

Creators are unstructured customer acquisition channels.

The opportunity is to take creator-driven demand and:

  • Standardize — unified processes and norms
  • Verify — confirm authenticity and quality
  • Rank — tier by performance
  • Route — intelligently match supply and demand
